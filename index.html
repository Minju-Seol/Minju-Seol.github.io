About Me

I am an integrated M.S./Ph.D Student in School of Mathematics (Computational Science and Engineering), Yonsei University and a member of Computational Methods in Medical Engineering (CMME) Lab. My research focuses on developing deep learning models for predicting abberation corrected phase and amplitude values in transcranial focused ultrasound in real time. I am interested in medical artificial intelligence, numerical simulation, and deep learning-based acoustic wave correction.

Research Interests

Medical Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning models for biomedical signal and image analysis.

Transcranial Focused Ultrasound

Acoustic simulation and real-time abberation correction for skull-induced distortion.

Computational Modeling

Numerical simulation, surrogate modeling, and physics-informed learning.

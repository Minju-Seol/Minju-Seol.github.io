About Me

I am an integrated M.S./Ph.D Student in School of Mathematics (Computational Science and Engineering), Yonsei University and a member of Computational Methods in Medical Engineering (CMME) Lab. My research focuses on developing deep learning models for predicting phase and amplitude correction values in transcranial focused ultrasound in real time. I am interested in biomedical engineering, medical artificial intelligence, numerical simulation, and deep learning-based acoustic wave correction.
